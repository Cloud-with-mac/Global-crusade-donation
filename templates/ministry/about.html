About Our Ministry

Transforming Lives Through Faith Since 1995

Our Story

Called to Reach the Nations

Global Crusade Ministry was born from a divine vision to see souls saved and lives transformed across the globe. Founded in 1995, we began with a single crusade that drew thousands, and God has continued to expand our reach ever since.

What started as a local outreach has grown into an international movement, touching lives in over 50 nations. Through the power of the Holy Spirit, we've witnessed countless miracles, healings, and salvations as we proclaim the Gospel with boldness and compassion.

Our ministry is built on the foundation of God's Word, empowered by prayer, and driven by a passion to see every person encounter the transforming love of Jesus Christ.
